Insurance

Call your insurance company with the phone numbers on the back.

Things to Know Before Your Appointment

There should be a 1-800 phone number on the back of your insurance card. Please call your insurance company to inquire if your plan has either or both of the following:

  1. Covers or includes acupuncture/chiropractic benefits and, if so, how many visits are covered per year.
  2. Co-pay, deductible, or referral requirements.
